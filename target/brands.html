Target Food is an agent to a wide variety of brands

Since its establishment in 2002, Target Food Co has proudly stood as one of Lebanon's foremost importers and distributors of superior quality products. Committed to serving the Lebanese market with utmost dedication, it offers an extensive selection of food and non-food items, including a diverse range of international and local household and sanitation products, confectionery delights, and a premium coffee collection.
