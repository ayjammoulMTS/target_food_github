About us

About Target Food

Founded in 2002, Target Food Co S.A.L. is committed to enriching the Lebanese market with superior, affordable food and non-food products from around the world. As a key importer and distributor, we ensure our wide-ranging products reach every corner of Lebanon. We operate under international standards, constantly enhancing our distribution and sales services to keep pace with consumer expectations. Our professional team conducts relentless market research, reinforcing our market leadership and our pledge to customer satisfaction.

Our Clients

Target Food Co S.A.L. has established robust partnerships with some of the most prominent supermarkets across Lebanon, bringing the world's flavors to your local aisles. Our unwavering commitment to quality and timely delivery ensures that these major retail outlets always have a fresh and varied range of our imported goods. These strong alliances reinforce our mission to make diverse, world-class products readily available for the Lebanese consumer.

In collaborating with these leading supermarkets, we further expand our reach and deepen our impact, catering to a larger audience. These partnerships stand testament to our reliability as a distributor and our ability to meet high-volume demand with efficiency and consistency. Not only do we bring a world of flavors to Lebanon, but we also contribute significantly to the vitality of the country's retail sector by ensuring a steady supply of high-quality, value-for-money products.
